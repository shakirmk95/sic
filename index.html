Objective

As part of Sukrita 17, IEDC TKMCE is planning to conduct a social Innovation Challenge, in which selected teams can pitch their socially innovative idea in front of a panel during Sukrita. The competition offers an opportunity for the participants to learn deeply their academic subjects and creatively apply the knowledge thus obtained to build a successful professional career along with social ethics.

Timeline
  • Registration Starting on November 2 using IEDC Website.
  • Last Date to Submit Abstract/Blue Print November 7.
  • A Panel Including Staff and Students Scrutinize the entries and announce the Selection results on November 8.
  • Teams Selected needs to submit their Pitch deck on November 11.
  • Selected teams can pitch their idea in front of the panel.
Rules
  • Teams Should follow the deadlines and important Dates.
  • Selection is based on Social relevance and depth of Innovation in the abstract submitted.
  • Preference for easily implementable ideas that can solve social issues in and around campus.
  • Each teams will be given 10 minutes of time to pitch their Idea.
  • Pitch Deck (PPT) should be submitted one day prior to the event. And can have a maximum of 8 Slides.
Topics
  • Technologies to safeguard life and properties during natural disasters
  • Technologies to tackle effects of climatic change
  • Women safety
  • Waste management
  • Digital literacy
  • E-governance
  • Educational tools
  • Renewable energy
  • Smart City projects
  • Technology to ease life of people in high population density areas
  • Smartphone based technologies made focusing on third world countries
  • Technologies to observe the effects of climate change
